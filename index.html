Humpback Whales and their interactions with Australian Waters

Data Visualisation 2 very rough draft

Where are the Whales?

Humpback Whales can be seen around Australia in different locations during different seasons.

Waters in which they Whale?

Whales have been recorded accross Australian waters. This shows the average sea surface temperature averaged annually and between 2000-2025. When this visualisation is complete I will overlay each season of the whale population distribution above over the temperature map to show how they migrate through different waters based on temperature and other factors.

The Ways of the Whales

Some records of whale sightings include a description of the activities they undertake. Most entries did not include this description.

Sea Surface Temperatures show a slight increasing trend since 1980.

Whale's migrate North, typically during winter to mate and give birth after spending the summer in Antarctic waters. Higher lattitudes haver warmer waters throughout the year.
